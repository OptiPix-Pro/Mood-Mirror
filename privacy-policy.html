Privacy Policy for Mood Mirror

Last Updated: May 22, 2024

We are committed to your privacy. All photo processing, meme creation, and emoji placement happens directly in your browser on your device. Your images are never uploaded to our servers. They remain on your computer or mobile device unless you choose to download or share the final meme.
