Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mood Mirror?

Mood Mirror is a free, browser-based tool that lets you create personalized memes in seconds. You can use your camera or upload a photo, add emojis and captions, and share your creation. Find out more on our About Us page.

Is it really free to use?

Yes, 100%! Mood Mirror is completely free. We support the site through unobtrusive ads. You can create and download as many memes as you like without any cost.

Are my photos private and secure?

Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority. All image processing happens directly in your browser. Your photos are never uploaded to our servers. Read our full Privacy Policy for more details.

How can I support Mood Mirror?

Thank you for asking! The best way to support us is by sharing the app with your friends. You can also send us feedback or feature ideas via email. We're a small team, and every bit of support helps us keep the tool free for everyone.
