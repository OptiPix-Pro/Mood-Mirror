About Mood Mirror

Welcome to Mood Mirror, the easiest and most fun way to turn your mood into a viral meme.

Our Mission

In a world of complex apps and privacy concerns, we wanted to create something simple, fun, and completely private. Our mission is to provide a free, accessible tool that lets you express yourself creatively without ever compromising your personal data.

How It Works

Mood Mirror is built on a "privacy-first" principle. When you use our app, everything happens right inside your web browser. Your photos are never sent to a server, and the memes you create are yours and yours alone.

Why Mood Mirror?

  • 100% Free: No subscriptions, no hidden fees. Just pure meme-making fun.
  • Completely Private: Your photos stay on your device. We never see or store them.
  • Super Easy: A clean, simple interface means you can go from photo to finished meme in seconds.
  • Instant Sharing: Download your creation or share it directly to your favorite social media platforms.

Thank you for using Mood Mirror. We hope it brings a little more fun and laughter to your day!
