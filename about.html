About Mood Mirror

Welcome to Mood Mirror, the easiest and most fun way to turn your mood into a viral meme. We believe that creativity should be simple, accessible, and private.

Our Mission

In a world of complex apps and privacy concerns, we wanted to create something different. Our mission is to provide a free, high-quality tool that lets you express yourself creatively without ever compromising your personal data. We're passionate about building a fun corner of the internet where you can make something cool in just a few clicks.

How It Works: Privacy First

Mood Mirror is built on a "privacy-first" principle. When you use our app, everything happens right inside your web browser. Your photos are never sent to a server, and the memes you create are yours and yours alone. This means you have complete control over your content from start to finish.

Why Mood Mirror?

  • 100% Free: No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no watermarks. Just pure meme-making fun.
  • Completely Private: Your photos stay on your device. We never see or store them. Period.
  • Super Easy: A clean, simple interface means you can go from photo to finished meme in seconds. No technical skills required.
  • Instant Sharing: Download your creation or use the share buttons to send it directly to your favorite social media platforms.

Support Our Project

Mood Mirror is a passion project run by a small team. We rely on ad revenue and user support to cover our costs and continue developing new features.

If you enjoy using the app, the best way to support us is by sharing it with your friends or providing feedback. For any questions, please visit our Contact & FAQ page.
